Farewell from Carloop

Carloop was a 100% open-source development kit designed to connect cars to the cloud, supporting applications like GPS tracking, mileage logging, and diagnostic trouble code decoding. It was introduced in 2016 and retired in 2024. While we no longer sell Carloop, our commitment to the community endures.

Our forum at community.carloop.io and our open-source repository at github.com/carloop remain active for enthusiasts and developers.

As passionate hackers, we encourage you to connect with us.
— Alan Mond & Julien Vanier, the Carloop team.
