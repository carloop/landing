Farewell from Carloop

Carloop was an innovative tool designed to bridge your car with the cloud, making vehicle data more accessible. While we've decided to discontinue sales, our spirit of innovation lives on.

We're keeping our community forum at community.carloop.io and our open-source repository at github.com/carloop active for enthusiasts and developers.

Our passion for hacking and innovation continues. Connect with us on LinkedIn or X (formerly Twitter) - Alan Mond & Julien Vanier are excited to explore future ventures with you.
