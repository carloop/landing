Farewell from Carloop

Carloop was a 100% open-source development kit designed to connect cars to the cloud, supporting applications like GPS tracking, mileage logging, and diagnostic trouble code decoding. While we no longer sell Carloop, our commitment to the community endures.

Our forum at community.carloop.io and our open-source repository at github.com/carloop remain active for enthusiasts and developers.

As passionate hackers, we encourage you to connect with us on LinkedIn or X (Formerly Twitter) — Alan Mond & Julien Vanier are looking forward to hearing from you.
